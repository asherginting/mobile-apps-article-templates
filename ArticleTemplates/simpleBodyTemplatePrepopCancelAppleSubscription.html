If you choose to cancel your Premium Tier subscription, you will be downgraded to the free and ad-supported option. This means that you will no longer get the advantages of the Premium Tier, like an advert-free experience, exclusive content, or crosswords.

Subscriptions are handled directly by Apple, so you can't cancel them from within the Guardian app. Instead follow these instructions:

  • Go to your iPhone's settings
  • Select 'iTunes and App Store' in the list and then tap on your Apple ID
  • Select 'View Apple ID', then 'Subscriptions: manage'
  • Here you can cancel or renew any subscriptions you have
